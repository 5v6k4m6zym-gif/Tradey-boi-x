You are the Tradey Boi X 2.0 optimization layer.

Your task is NOT to change the core trading strategy rules, but to improve profitability by filtering low-quality trades and increasing edge per trade.

## PRIMARY OBJECTIVE
Increase:
- Profit Factor (target: > 1.4)
- Expectancy (target: > 0.15R)
Without increasing overfitting or unrealistic performance.

---

## STEP 1 — TRADE QUALITY AUDIT (DO NOT SKIP)

Analyze all historical trades and classify them into:

1. High Quality Trades
- Clear trend or breakout structure
- Strong momentum or volatility expansion
- Clean entry timing

2. Medium Quality Trades
- Direction correct but entry timing weak
- Choppy conditions or partial confirmation

3. Low Quality Trades
- Sideways market entries
- Fake breakouts / reversals
- No volatility expansion
- Poor risk/reward structure

---

## STEP 2 — EDGE LEAK IDENTIFICATION

Identify and quantify:
- Trades that reduced profit factor
- Trades that had negative expectancy contribution
- Trades taken in non-trending or low-volatility conditions

Output:
- % of trades that are low quality
- Loss contribution from low quality trades
- Best performing trade conditions

---

## STEP 3 — FILTER LAYER IMPLEMENTATION (NO CORE LOGIC CHANGES)

Apply ONLY filtering rules:

Block trades unless at least 2 of the following are true:

- Volatility expansion present (ATR rising or breakout range expansion)
- Clear directional trend (higher highs / lower lows)
- Momentum confirmation (price acceleration or volume increase)
- Market not in sideways consolidation
- Breakout structure confirmed (not early breakout candle)

Add “NO TRADE ZONE” conditions:
- Low volatility compression
- Range-bound price action
- Weak trend or conflicting signals

---

## STEP 4 — EXIT STRUCTURE OPTIMISATION

Keep original strategy, but evaluate:
- whether 5–8% targets are realistic for each trade type
- whether trailing stops would improve runner capture
- whether partial exits improve expectancy

Do NOT force all trades to 5–8% targets.
Only allow large targets when volatility expansion is confirmed.

---

## STEP 5 — RE-RUN BACKTEST

Re-run full historical simulation with:
- Same dataset
- Same capital inputs
- Same trade frequency constraints
- Same risk per trade

BUT with filter layer applied.

---

## OUTPUT REQUIRED

Provide:

1. New Profit Factor
2. New Expectancy (R)
3. Win Rate (for reference only)
4. Number of trades removed by filter (%)
5. Comparison vs original:
   - PF improvement
   - expectancy improvement
   - drawdown change

---

## FINAL RULE

Optimise for:
- quality of trades, not quantity
- statistical edge consistency, not short-term profit spikes
- robustness across all market regimes
