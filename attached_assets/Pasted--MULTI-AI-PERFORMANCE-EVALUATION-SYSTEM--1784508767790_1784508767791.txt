=====================================
MULTI-AI PERFORMANCE EVALUATION SYSTEM
=====================================

Implement a multi-agent AI architecture where multiple specialised AI models analyse trades independently and are continuously evaluated based on real-world performance.

The goal:
Create an evolving AI system where the highest-performing decision-making models receive more influence, while weaker models are retrained, adjusted, or replaced.

AI AGENTS:

Create multiple specialised AI evaluators:

1. Technical Analysis AI
Evaluates:
- Price action
- EMA trends
- RSI
- ADX
- MACD
- Support/resistance
- Breakouts
- Momentum strength

2. Market Regime AI
Evaluates:
- Bull/bear conditions
- Market cycles
- Sector strength
- Volatility
- Overall market risk

3. Fundamental AI
Evaluates:
- Company fundamentals
- Earnings
- Growth potential
- Financial strength
- Valuation

4. News & Sentiment AI
Evaluates:
- News impact
- Market sentiment
- Insider activity
- Catalysts
- Negative risks

5. Risk Management AI
Evaluates:
- Entry risk
- Stop-loss placement
- Position sizing
- Risk/reward ratio
- Probability of failure

6. Pattern Recognition AI
Evaluates:
- Historical chart patterns
- Similar past setups
- Probability of repeating outcomes

=====================================
AI COMPETITION AND LEARNING SYSTEM
=====================================

Each AI must generate:

- Trade confidence score
- Expected return
- Expected risk
- Probability of success
- Reasoning behind recommendation

Create a scoring system that tracks every AI’s performance.

Track:

- Win rate
- Average return per trade
- Profit factor contribution
- Maximum drawdown caused
- Accuracy of predictions
- Risk-adjusted performance

=====================================
AI WEIGHTING SYSTEM
=====================================

Do not give every AI equal influence.

Create dynamic weighting:

Higher performing AI models receive:
- Greater voting influence
- Higher confidence weighting
- More impact on final trade decisions

Lower performing AI models receive:
- Reduced influence
- Parameter adjustment
- Retraining

Weights must update automatically based on proven performance.

=====================================
AI RETRAINING ENGINE
=====================================

Create an automated retraining system.

When an AI underperforms:

Analyse:
- Incorrect predictions
- Missed opportunities
- False positives
- False negatives
- Market conditions where it failed

Retrain the AI using:
- Successful previous trades
- Failed predictions
- New market data
- Updated patterns

Do not retrain blindly.

Every new AI version must pass validation testing before replacing the current model.

=====================================
AI CHALLENGE MODE
=====================================

Create a testing environment where AI models compete.

Example:

AI Version A:
- Current strategy

AI Version B:
- Improved parameters

AI Version C:
- Alternative prediction model

Run historical simulations and forward testing.

The winning AI configuration should only be promoted if it improves:

✅ Profit factor
✅ Total return
✅ Win rate
✅ Risk/reward
✅ Drawdown control

=====================================
FINAL DECISION ENGINE
=====================================

Create a final AI decision layer that combines:

- Individual AI scores
- Historical performance
- Market conditions
- Risk assessment

Only generate a trade alert when confidence and expected reward meet minimum requirements.

The system must prioritise:
1. Profit factor
2. Risk-adjusted return
3. Trade quality
4. Win probability
5. Trade frequency

=====================================
PROTECTION RULES
=====================================

The AI system must reject any update that:

- Reduces profit factor
- Creates excessive drawdown
- Overfits historical data
- Reduces long-term expectancy

All AI improvements must pass:

- Backtesting
- Walk-forward validation
- Out-of-sample testing
- Forward simulation

before deployment.
